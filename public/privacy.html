Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

The short version: Play as a guest and nothing is collected — your progress lives on your device only. Sign in and we store the minimum needed to sync your account across devices: email, username, scores, and game history. The app shows no ads and runs no usage tracking. Our website (balliq.app) shows ads via Google, which uses cookies, alongside privacy-friendly cookieless analytics and Microsoft Clarity session analytics — but the app itself stays ad-free and cookie-free. We never sell your data.

1. Two ways to play

Guest mode — open Ball IQ without signing in. Your scores, streaks, settings and game history are stored only in your device's local storage. Nothing is sent to our servers. If you clear your browser data or uninstall the app, that progress is gone.

Signed-in mode — sign in with email to back up your progress and play online multiplayer. We store the minimum needed to deliver those features (detail in §2 below). You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Account → Delete Account, which removes everything we hold about you.

2. What we store when you sign in

When you have an account, the following lives on our servers (hosted by Supabase, see §5):

  • Email address — used only for sign-in (password authentication) and account-confirmation email at signup. Never used for marketing.
  • Username + avatar — visible to other signed-in players in friend search and friend profiles.
  • Game results — Daily 7 scores, Footle history, total games played, total XP, best streaks, and the questions you got wrong (for the review screen).
  • Friendships — friend requests sent + accepted, so your friends list works across devices.
  • Onboarded flag — a single timestamp so we don't replay onboarding on every device.

That's the full list. We do not collect your real name, location, device fingerprint, or any contact list. We never sell or share this data with third parties for marketing.

3. Error monitoring

We use Sentry to catch crashes and bugs. When something goes wrong in the app, Sentry receives a stack trace, the browser version, and a short trail of recent actions ("breadcrumbs") — no question text, no personal data. We strip your email and auth tokens from every event before it leaves your device. We use this only to fix bugs, never for tracking.

4. Analytics & ads

The Ball IQ app uses no analytics tools, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs. It does not measure how you use it, shows no ads, and works with no ad networks. This is enforced in code: the ad and analytics scripts are blocked from ever loading inside the app.

Our website (balliq.app) uses Vercel Web Analytics — a privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics service — to understand aggregate traffic, such as how many people visit a page. It sets no cookies, does not track you across other websites, and does not identify you personally.

Our website (balliq.app) also uses Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors interact with the site — it captures anonymised interaction signals (clicks, scrolls, mouse movement) and session replays with typed text masked, which we use to find and fix confusing parts of the site. Clarity may set cookies and similar identifiers. This runs on the website only — never in the app. See Microsoft's privacy statement at privacy.microsoft.com.

Our website (balliq.app) also displays ads through Google AdSense. To serve and measure ads, Google and its partners may set and read cookies or similar identifiers in your browser, and — where you agree — use them to show more relevant ads. This applies to the website only; the app remains ad-free and sets no advertising cookies. If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you will be asked for your consent choices through a Google-certified consent prompt before any personalised ads are shown, and you can change your choice at any time. You can review how Google uses data from sites that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites, and manage ad personalisation at adssettings.google.com.

5. Third-party services we use

  • Supabase (database, auth, storage): When you sign in, your email, username, profile data, game results and avatar image are stored on Supabase servers (EU region). Supabase is our data processor.
  • Sentry (error monitoring): Crash reports are sent to Sentry with email and tokens scrubbed before transmission.
  • Vercel (hosting + website analytics): Serves Ball IQ over HTTPS and may log standard request data (IP, timestamp, URL) for operational purposes. On our website (balliq.app) we also use Vercel Web Analytics for aggregate, cookieless usage metrics — no cookies, no cross-site tracking, no personal identification.
  • Google AdSense (website ads): Our website (balliq.app) displays ads through Google AdSense. Google and its ad partners may set cookies or similar identifiers to serve, measure, and (with consent) personalise ads. This runs on the website only — never in the app. See policies.google.com/technologies/ads.
  • Microsoft Clarity (website session analytics): Captures anonymised interaction data on balliq.app (clicks, scrolls, session replays with typed text masked) to help us improve the site. May set cookies. Website only — never in the app. See privacy.microsoft.com.
  • Google Fonts: We load the Inter and JetBrains Mono fonts from fonts.googleapis.com. Google may log your IP address when fonts are fetched.
  • Cropper.js (CDN): When you upload a profile picture, we load an image-cropping library from cdnjs.cloudflare.com. The CDN provider may log your IP address.

6. Children's Privacy

Ball IQ is not directed at children under 13. Guest mode requires no account or personal data. To sign in you must provide an email address — please don't sign in if you are under 13. The app's content is suitable for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
