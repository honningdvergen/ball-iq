Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

The short version: Ball IQ does not collect, store or share any personal data. All your game progress is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

1. Information We Collect

Ball IQ does not collect any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any identifying information to use the app.

2. Data Storage

All app data — including your scores, XP, badges, settings and game history — is stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never transmitted to our servers
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser or app data

3. Analytics

Ball IQ does not use any analytics tools, tracking pixels, or third-party SDKs that collect usage data. We do not track how you use the app.

4. Advertising

Ball IQ does not display advertisements and does not work with any advertising networks.

5. Third-Party Services

Ball IQ uses Google Fonts (Inter and JetBrains Mono) to display text. This means your device makes a request to Google's servers to download these fonts. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle font requests.

No other third-party services are used.

6. Children's Privacy

Ball IQ does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no inappropriate content and is suitable for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: privacy@ball-iq.app
